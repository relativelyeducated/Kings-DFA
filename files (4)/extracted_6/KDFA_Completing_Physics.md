**What it misses: How to couple with force-based description**

**What it captures: Spacetime geometry, gravitational effects**

**What it misses: Quantum phenomena, probabilistic behavior**

**iℏ(∂ψ/∂t) = Ĥψ**

Probabilistic, discrete, featuring superposition and entanglement. Yet
