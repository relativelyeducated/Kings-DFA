## 3. Statistical Methods
### Power Law Verification (Primary Signature)
For each system, we will analyze the distribution of the Relational (R) variable (e.g., distance, energy, connectivity):
1.  **Fit**: Fit data to Power Law (\(P(x) \propto x^{-\alpha}\)), Exponential, and Lognormal distributions.
2.  **Test**: Use Log-Likelihood Ratio (LLR) to determine if Power Law is the *best* fit.
3.  **Recursive Test**: Apply to nested subsystems (e.g., Galaxy -> Cluster -> Supercluster).

## 4. Success Criteria
- **Strong Validation**: \(\Omega\) values match within 0.5%, \(D_2\) matches within 1%, Power Law is best fit with \(p < 0.05\).
