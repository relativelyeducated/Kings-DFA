# Claim: Stellar Formation Threshold

## Source
Grok Export 031

## Statement
Formation threshold mass:
\[
M_* = \frac{4\pi G \rho_0 R^3}{3} \cdot \exp\left( -\frac{\alpha}{N} \cdot \log(R / R_J) \right)
\]
where \(\rho_0\) is Jeans density.

## Predictions
For \(R = 10\) pc, predicts \(M_* \approx 0.8 M_\odot\), aligning with IMF peaks.
