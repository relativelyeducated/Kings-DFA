## Validation Methodology

### Status Definitions

- **VALIDATED**: Quantitative prediction matches observation within stated error bounds, confirmed by independent data
- **PEER-REVIEW READY**: Strong evidence with <10% error, suitable for publication, awaiting formal peer review
- **THEORETICAL**: Theoretical prediction with qualitative support or preliminary evidence, awaiting rigorous testing

### Error Thresholds

- **Exact**: <0.5% deviation
- **Strong**: 0.5-3% deviation
- **Moderate**: 3-10% deviation
- **Qualitative**: Pattern/trend match without precise quantification

### Evidence Hierarchy

1. **Direct experimental measurement** (metallic glass, Planck cosmology)
2. **Established theoretical model** (She-Leveque, Salpeter IMF)
3. **Statistical validation** (Monte Carlo for stellar heartbeat)
4. **Historical correlation** (elite wealth thresholds)
5. **Simulation/modeling** (tension-driven mutation)
6. **Qualitative alignment** (protein folding, life emergence)
