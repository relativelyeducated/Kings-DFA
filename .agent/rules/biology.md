# Biology Domain Rules

## General Biology Knowledge
- **Protein Folding**: Misfolding, aggregation, energy landscapes.
- **Chaperone Systems**: HSP40, HSP70, HSP90, HSP104 functions and cycles.
- **Proteostasis Network**: Synthesis, folding, degradation balance.
- **Prion Diseases**: Amyloidosis, neurodegeneration mechanisms.
- **Cell Signaling**: Apoptosis pathways, stress responses.
- **Metabolic Networks**: Enzyme kinetics, flux balance.
- **Evolutionary Dynamics**: Fitness landscapes, mutation rates.
- **Allosteric Regulation**: Conformational changes, binding sites.
- **Simulation Methods**: Molecular dynamics, Monte Carlo.
- **Structure Analysis**: PDB file format, Ramachandran plots.

## DFA-Specific Extensions
- **D₂ Analysis**: Fractal dimension analysis of protein structures (backbone/sidechains).
- **Healthy vs Pathological**: D₂ signatures distinguishing functional vs aggregated states.
- **Chaperone Failure**: Interpreted as a zone transition (Zone 2 -> Zone 3).
