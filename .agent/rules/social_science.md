# Social Science Domain Rules

## General Social Science Knowledge
- **Complex Adaptive Systems**: Emergence, self-organization in societies.
- **Network Theory**: Scale-free networks, small-world properties.
- **Power Laws**: Wealth distribution (Pareto), city sizes (Zipf).
- **Institutional Economics**: Transaction costs, property rights.
- **Collapse Theories**: Tainter (diminishing returns), Turchin (secular cycles).
- **Cliodynamics**: Mathematical modeling of historical dynamics.
- **Diffusion**: Innovation adoption curves.
- **Phase Transitions**: Social tipping points, revolutions.
- **Game Theory**: Prisoner's dilemma, cooperation dynamics.
- **Demographics**: Transition theory, population momentum.
