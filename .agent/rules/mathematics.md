# Mathematics Domain Rules

## General Mathematical Knowledge
- **Dynamical Systems**: Stability, bifurcations, attractors.
- **Information Theory**: Shannon entropy, mutual information.
- **Fractal Geometry**: Hausdorff dimension, box-counting dimension.
- **Optimization**: Lagrangian multipliers, variational calculus.
- **Stochastic Processes**: Markov chains, random walks.
- **Category Theory**: Functors, natural transformations (basics).
- **Renormalization**: Scaling groups, critical exponents.

## DFA-Specific Extensions
- **S-R Axis Formalism**: Mathematical definition of the Stimulus-Response axes.
- **Kappa Derivations**: Derivation of the coupling constant κ.
- **D₂-κ Relationship**: Theoretical link between fractal dimension and coupling.
