# Neuroscience Domain Rules

## General Neuroscience Knowledge
- **Neural Oscillations**: EEG frequency bands (alpha, beta, gamma, etc.).
- **Criticality**: Neuronal avalanches, power laws in neural networks.
- **Consciousness Theories**: IIT (Tononi), GWT (Baars/Dehaene), Predictive Processing (Friston).
- **Anesthesia**: Mechanisms of action, loss of consciousness.
- **Sleep**: NREM/REM stages, transitions, replay.
- **Pathology**: Epilepsy, seizure dynamics, excitotoxicity.
- **Imaging**: fMRI BOLD signals, connectivity analysis.
- **Neurotransmitters**: Glutamate/GABA balance, neuromodulation.
- **Quantum Theories**: Penrose-Hameroff Orch-OR (for comparison).
